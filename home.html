ABOUT
OUR
FARM

Our farm is a family-friendly destination that specializes in the cultivation of delicious strawberries and blueberries. We offer a fun and educational experience for visitors of all ages, with plenty of opportunities for hands-on activities like berry picking and farm tours. Our goal is to provide a relaxing and enjoyable atmosphere where families can come together to create memories and experience the joy of farming.

The staff was incredibly welcoming and made our experience all the more enjoyable. It was a great bonding experience for us, and I can't wait to come back with her and continue making memories at the farm.
