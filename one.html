ABOUT US

3rd Generation Family-Owned Business

Lambert Family Berry Farm is a third-generation family-owned business that has been operating for over 50 years. Situated in the heart of the lush countryside, the farm boasts acres of sprawling land that is dedicated to growing some of the finest and most delicious berries around. The farm has been passed down from generation to generation, with each new family member adding their own unique touch to the business. Today, the farm is run by siblings Tom and Emily Lambert, who have worked tirelessly to ensure that their family's legacy lives on through their commitment to quality and sustainable farming practices. Their mission is simple: to provide the freshest, most flavorful berries possible, while also caring for the land and preserving the natural beauty of the area. Whether you're a local resident or a visitor passing through, a trip to Lambert Family Berry Farm is sure to be a memorable experience that you won't soon forget.







About Lambert Farm

Lambert Farm is a unique and vibrant berry farm located in the heart of the countryside. We take great pride in the way we grow our fruits, using only the best and most sustainable practices. Our berries are grown without any harmful pesticides or chemicals, allowing them to develop their natural sweetness and flavour. We believe that our approach to farming not only produces the best-tasting fruits but also ensures that our environment remains healthy and vibrant for generations to come.

At Lambert Farm, we offer a range of activities for visitors to enjoy, from picking their berries to taking a stroll through our beautiful orchards. Our staff is knowledgeable and passionate about our farm and is always on hand to answer any questions visitors may have. We also offer educational tours for schools and groups, allowing people to learn about the process of growing and harvesting berries and gain an appreciation for the hard work that goes into producing such delicious and nutritious fruits.

What sets Lambert Farm apart is our commitment to sustainability and community. We are constantly striving to improve our farming practices, and we work closely with local organizations and charities to give back to the community. Our visitors not only get to enjoy delicious fruits and fun activities, but they also get to support a company that is making a positive impact on the world around us. We are proud of who we are and what we do, and we welcome everyone to come and experience the Lambert Farm difference for themselves.
