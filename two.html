ACTIVITIES

Pick Your Own Strawberries

Description

Looking for a fun and tasty outdoor activity? Come visit our Pick Your Own Strawberries experience!

We invite you to take a stroll through our beautiful fields and pick your own ripe and juicy strawberries straight from the plant. Our family-friendly environment is perfect for all ages, making it an ideal outing for families, friends, or even a romantic date. Our carefully tended fields are filled with only the freshest and most delicious strawberries, ensuring that you'll have a delicious and healthy snack or ingredient for your next recipe. You'll love the satisfaction of picking your own berries, and you can be confident that you're getting the very best quality. So come and join us for a day of fresh air and berry picking! Our friendly staff will be on hand to answer any questions you may have and to make sure you have a fun and enjoyable experience. We look forward to seeing you soon!

Price: $5/hr

From Farm to Table:
Learn How to Grow Your Own Strawberries and Blueberries

Discription

Are you interested in cultivating your own delicious and nutritious fruits at home? Our "From Farm to Table" workshop offers you the opportunity to learn how to grow strawberries and blueberries in your own backyard!

In this hands-on workshop, you'll get expert guidance from our experienced farmers on how to plant, care for, and harvest your own berries. You'll learn about soil preparation, fertilization, pest control, and other essential skills to help you get the most out of your berry plants. Not only will you get to enjoy the satisfaction of growing your own food, but you'll also learn about the health benefits and culinary uses of these superfoods. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a novice, this workshop is a fun and informative activity for all.

So come and join us to discover the joy of growing your own strawberries and blueberries. You'll leave with the knowledge and confidence to start your own home garden and enjoy the delicious and healthy fruits of your labor!

Price: $30
