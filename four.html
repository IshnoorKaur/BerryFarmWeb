Visitor Policy

To promote food safety, security, defense, employee safety, and well-being, access to our farm property, including fields, facilities, and equipment, is controlled. Therefore, our farm has implemented specific policies regarding visitors.
Visitors are defined as individuals who are not farm owners or designated workers. Before touring any properties, visitors must obtain permission from the farm owner. Signs will be posted at the entrances of our farms stating:

  • "No Trespassing"
  • "No Soliciting"
  • "All Visitors must call 240-372-0674. PICK-YOUR-OWN/FARM CUSTOMERS/DELIVERIES MAY PROCEED TO BARN AT AUTHORIZED TIMES."

Fields and work areas are not accessible to unapproved visitors, except for those fields authorized for access during approved pick-your-own appointments. Approved visitors must wear identifying gear when entering work areas. Glass or glass cups are not permitted.


Visitors must comply with all posted signs, which may include:

  • "No Trespassing! No Soliciting!"
  • "Contact the main office at 240-372-0674"
  • "Entering a Restricted Area - No children, animals, or unauthorized personnel"
  • "Do not enter without permission - Only employees on duty can enter"
  • "No Chewing Gum"
  • "No Smoking"
  • "All visitors must abide by farm rules and hygiene requirements"
  • "Danger - Chemical Storage"
  • "Danger - Pesticide."
